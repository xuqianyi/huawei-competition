Our Services

We provide a platform designed to enhance self-learning ability in ECG interpretation that includes 3 parts: learn, test, and personalized study plan for premium user.

Learn

Provide multimedia tutorials in ECG interpretation including text, plots, heart sounds and dynamic ECG plots in user friendly arrangement.

Test

Provide general test with random questions from our huge question bank. Choose your desired number of questions to answer.

Study Plan

Customized study plan with learning and testing arrangements and test report for each general test with customized review materials

About Us

We want to build an application that aims to teach young residence and medical students to read ECG. We hope to provide a self-learning platform that can give them more access to real-world ECG data and more clinical cases. This application provides text and illustration teaching as well as quizzes for testing. Users will be tested to read ECG and give their diagnosis. To expand the question pool, we intend to use generative AI models to produce synthetic ECG and allow patients to upload their medical records. Users can give feedback on incorrect AI-generated answers and the answer will be updated after investigation.
